  • Backward Compatibility of Releases

    The MADlib project uses Semantic Versioning. All versions, starting with MADlib v1.0, will be API compliant and we will supply mechanisms for upgrading from prior versions to the current active version until the release of MADlib v2.0 which may have different API. In many cases this may mean that a particular function may become deprecated, yet still remain callable, until a later major version is released.



MADlib is an effort undergoing incubation at The Apache Software Foundation (ASF), sponsored by the Incubator. Incubation is required of all newly accepted projects until a further review indicates that the infrastructure, communications, and decision making process have stabilized in a manner consistent with other successful ASF projects. While incubation status is not necessarily a reflection of the completeness or stability of the code, it does indicate that the project has yet to be fully endorsed by the ASF.
